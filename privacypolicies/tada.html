Privacy Policy

At TaDa, we value your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. Please read it carefully to understand our approach.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. TaDa works entirely offline, ensuring your privacy is respected at all times. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

TaDa does not require any special permissions beyond what is necessary for core functionality. We do not access your contacts, media, or other personal information.

Offline Usage

All functionalities in TaDa are designed to work offline. This means you can manage your tasks without any need for an internet connection, ensuring your data stays with you.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. You have complete control over your data, with options to backup, restore, or clear all data whenever you need.

Backup and Restore

TaDa provides backup and restore options to help you manage your data securely. Backups are stored on your device, and no external parties have access to them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be communicated through the app, ensuring you're always informed about how your data is managed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at: support@tadaapp.com
